# 1. Title: Describing a Friend

**Anna:** Who is your best friend?
**Tom:** My best friend is Jack.
**Anna:** What does he look like?
**Tom:** He is tall and thin.
**Anna:** Does he have black hair?
**Tom:** Yes, he has short black hair and brown eyes.
**Anna:** Is he friendly?
**Tom:** Yes, he is very friendly and funny.

# 2. Title: Describing a Teacher

**Lina:** Who is your English teacher?
**Ben:** Her name is Mrs. Brown.
**Lina:** What is she like?
**Ben:** She is kind and patient.
**Lina:** What does she look like?
**Ben:** She has long blonde hair and glasses.
**Lina:** Is she strict?
**Ben:** A little, but she is nice.

# 3. Title: Describing a Family Member

**Mia:** Tell me about your father.
**John:** My father is tall and strong.
**Mia:** What does he do?
**John:** He is a doctor.
**Mia:** Does he wear glasses?
**John:** Yes, he does.
**Mia:** Is he busy?
**John:** Yes, but he always spends time with our family.

# 4. Title: Describing a Classmate

**Lucy:** Who sits next to you in class?
**David:** A girl named Emma.
**Lucy:** What does she look like?
**David:** She has curly brown hair and blue eyes.
**Lucy:** Is she quiet?
**David:** No, she is very talkative and cheerful.

# 5. Title: Describing a Neighbor

**Paul:** Do you know your new neighbor?
**Sara:** Yes, I do.
**Paul:** What is he like?
**Sara:** He is old but very kind.
**Paul:** What does he look like?
**Sara:** He has white hair and a beard.
**Paul:** Is he friendly?
**Sara:** Yes, he always smiles and says hello.

# 6. Title: Describing a Celebrity
