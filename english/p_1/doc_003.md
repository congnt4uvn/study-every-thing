# 1. Good morning at Home

**Mom:** Good morning! Did you sleep well?
**Son:** Yes, Mom. I slept very well.
**Mom:** Great. Breakfast is ready.
**Son:** Thank you! I’m hungry.

---

# 2. Good morning at School

**Teacher:** Good morning, class.
**Students:** Good morning, teacher.
**Teacher:** How are you today?
**Students:** We are fine, thank you.

---

# 3. Good morning at Work

**Anna:** Good morning, Tom.
**Tom:** Good morning, Anna.
**Anna:** Are you ready for the meeting?
**Tom:** Yes, I am ready.

---

# 4. Good morning at a Coffee Shop

**Customer:** Good morning. Can I have a coffee?
**Barista:** Good morning! Sure. Small or large?
**Customer:** Small, please.
**Barista:** Okay. Here you go.

---

# 5. Good morning with Friends

**Lisa:** Good morning, Jenny!
**Jenny:** Good morning! How are you?
**Lisa:** I’m good. Want to go for a walk?
**Jenny:** Yes, let’s go.

# 6. Good morning on the Bus

**Man:** Good morning. Is this seat free?
**Woman:** Yes, it is.
**Man:** Thank you.
**Woman:** You’re welcome.
