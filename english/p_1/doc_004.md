# 1. Good afternoon at School

**Teacher:** Good afternoon, everyone.
**Students:** Good afternoon, teacher.
**Teacher:** Are you ready for the lesson?
**Students:** Yes, we are.

---

# 2. Good afternoon at Work

**Manager:** Good afternoon, David.
**David:** Good afternoon.
**Manager:** Can you finish the report today?
**David:** Yes, I can.

---

# 3. Good afternoon at a Restaurant

**Waiter:** Good afternoon. Welcome!
**Customer:** Good afternoon. A table for two, please.
**Waiter:** Sure. This way, please.
**Customer:** Thank you.

---

# 4. Good afternoon with Friends

**Mia:** Good afternoon, Lily.
**Lily:** Good afternoon!
**Mia:** Do you want some coffee?
**Lily:** Yes, please.

---

# 5. Good afternoon at the Park

**Boy:** Good afternoon, Mr. Brown.
**Mr. Brown:** Good afternoon, Sam.
**Boy:** Nice weather today.
**Mr. Brown:** Yes, it is very sunny.

---

# 6. Good afternoon on the Phone

**Anna:** Good afternoon, John speaking.
**Mark:** Good afternoon, John. This is Mark.
**Anna:** How can I help you?
**Mark:** I want to ask about the meeting.

---

# 7. Good afternoon at Home

**Mom:** Good afternoon! Are you hungry?
**Daughter:** Yes, a little.
**Mom:** I made some sandwiches.
**Daughter:** Great, thank you!

---

# 8. Good afternoon at the Store

**Cashier:** Good afternoon.
**Customer:** Good afternoon. How much is this?
**Cashier:** It’s five dollars.
**Customer:** Okay, I’ll take it.

---

# 9. Good afternoon at the Library

**Librarian:** Good afternoon. Can I help you?
**Student:** Yes, I need an English book.
**Librarian:** Please follow me.
**Student:** Thank you very much.

---

# 10. Good afternoon Before a Meeting

**Emma:** Good afternoon, Chris.
**Chris:** Good afternoon, Emma.
**Emma:** Is the meeting starting soon?
**Chris:** Yes, in five minutes.
