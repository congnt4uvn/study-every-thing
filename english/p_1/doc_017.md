# Interview in a Company – Sample Conversations

## 1. First Job Interview

**Interviewer:** Good morning. Please introduce yourself.
**Candidate:** Good morning. My name is Anna. I recently graduated from university.

**Interviewer:** What did you study?
**Candidate:** I studied marketing.

**Interviewer:** Why do you want this job?
**Candidate:** I want to learn new skills and grow my career.

**Interviewer:** Do you have experience?
**Candidate:** Yes, I worked as an intern for three months.

**Interviewer:** Thank you for coming today.
**Candidate:** Thank you for your time.

---

## 2. Interview for Office Assistant

**Interviewer:** Tell me about yourself.
**Candidate:** I am friendly, organized, and hardworking.

**Interviewer:** Can you use Microsoft Office?
**Candidate:** Yes, I can use Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.

**Interviewer:** How do you handle pressure?
**Candidate:** I stay calm and finish tasks step by step.

**Interviewer:** Why should we hire you?
**Candidate:** Because I learn quickly and work well with people.

---

## 3. Interview for Restaurant Job

**Manager:** Do you have experience in restaurants?
**Applicant:** Yes, I worked in a café for one year.

**Manager:** Can you work on weekends?
**Applicant:** Yes, I can.

**Manager:** How do you treat customers?
**Applicant:** I always speak politely and help customers quickly.

**Manager:** When can you start?
**Applicant:** I can start next Monday.

---

## 4. Online Job Interview

**Interviewer:** Can you hear me clearly?
**Candidate:** Yes, I can hear you well.

**Interviewer:** Why are you interested in our company?
**Candidate:** Your company has a good reputation and friendly environment.

**Interviewer:** What are your strengths?
**Candidate:** I am responsible and good at teamwork.

**Interviewer:** Do you have any questions for us?
**Candidate:** Yes. What training do you provide for new employees?

---

## 5. Interview for a Software Company

**Interviewer:** What programming languages do you know?
**Candidate:** I know Python, JavaScript, and Java.

**Interviewer:** What projects have you worked on?
**Candidate:** I created a small website and a mobile app.

**Interviewer:** Can you work in a team?
**Candidate:** Yes, I enjoy working with others.

**Interviewer:** Why do you want to join our company?
**Candidate:** I want to improve my skills and work on real projects.

---

## 6. Interview for Sales Position

**Manager:** Do you like talking with customers?
**Applicant:** Yes, I enjoy helping people.

**Manager:** How do you sell a product?
**Applicant:** I listen to the customer’s needs first.

**Manager:** What is your biggest strength?
**Applicant:** I am confident and patient.

**Manager:** Thank you for coming.
**Applicant:** Thank you very much.

---

## 7. Interview for Hotel Receptionist

**Interviewer:** Why do you want to work in a hotel?
**Candidate:** I like meeting new people and helping guests.

**Interviewer:** Can you speak English well?
**Candidate:** Yes, I can communicate in English.

**Interviewer:** How would you help an angry guest?
**Candidate:** I would stay calm and solve the problem politely.

**Interviewer:** That sounds good.
**Candidate:** Thank you.

---

## 8. Interview for Internship

**Interviewer:** Are you still studying?
**Student:** Yes, I am in my final year at university.

**Interviewer:** Why do you want this internship?
**Student:** I want practical experience.

**Interviewer:** What are your career goals?
**Student:** I want to become a professional engineer.

**Interviewer:** Great. We will contact you soon.
**Student:** Thank you very much.

---

## 9. Interview About Teamwork

**Interviewer:** Do you prefer working alone or in a team?
**Candidate:** I like both, but I enjoy teamwork more.

**Interviewer:** Why?
**Candidate:** Because people can share ideas and help each other.

**Interviewer:** Have you worked in a team before?
**Candidate:** Yes, many times at school and work.

---

## 10. Final Interview

**Manager:** What salary do you expect?
**Candidate:** I am open to discussion.

**Manager:** Why are you leaving your current job?
**Candidate:** I want new challenges and opportunities.

**Manager:** When can you join us?
**Candidate:** I can join next month.

**Manager:** Thank you. We will inform you soon.
**Candidate:** Thank you for the interview.
