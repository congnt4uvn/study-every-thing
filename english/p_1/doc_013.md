# 1. Meeting New People at a Party

**Anna:** Hi, I’m Anna.
**Lisa:** Nice to meet you, Anna. I’m Lisa.
**Anna:** Is this your first time here?
**Lisa:** Yes, it is.

---

# 2. Introducing Yourself

**Tom:** Hello, my name is Tom.
**Ben:** Hi Tom, I’m Ben.
**Tom:** Where are you from?
**Ben:** I’m from Canada.

---

# 3. Talking About Hobbies

**Emma:** What do you like to do in your free time?
**Lucy:** I like reading and painting.
**Emma:** That sounds interesting.
**Lucy:** What about you?

---

# 4. At an English Class

**Teacher:** Please talk with your partner.
**Student 1:** Hi, I’m Kevin.
**Student 2:** Nice to meet you, Kevin. I’m Chris.
**Kevin:** Nice to meet you too.

---

# 5. At Work

**Manager:** This is Sarah, our new coworker.
**Mike:** Nice to meet you, Sarah.
**Sarah:** Nice to meet you too.
**Mike:** Welcome to the team.

---

# 6. Asking Simple Questions

**Ryan:** How old are you?
**Jack:** I’m 22 years old.
**Ryan:** Are you a student?
**Jack:** Yes, I am.

---

# 7. Talking at a Coffee Shop

**Girl:** Is this seat free?
**Boy:** Yes, please sit down.
**Girl:** Thank you. I’m Mia.
**Boy:** Nice to meet you, Mia. I’m Daniel.

---

# 8. Meeting a Neighbor

**Woman:** Hello, I’m your new neighbor.
**Man:** Nice to meet you. I’m John.
**Woman:** I just moved here yesterday.
**Man:** Welcome to the neighborhood.

---

# 9. At the Gym

**Kevin:** Do you come here often?
**Chris:** Yes, three times a week.
**Kevin:** I’m new here.
**Chris:** That’s great. This gym is very good.

---

# 10. Saying Goodbye to New Friends

**Anna:** It was nice meeting you today.
**Lisa:** Nice meeting you too.
**Anna:** See you again soon.
**Lisa:** I hope so!
