# 1. Greeting a Friend

**A:** Hello, how are you?
**B:** I’m good, thank you. How are you?
**A:** I’m fine too.
**B:** Nice to see you!

---

# 2. Meeting at School

**A:** Hello, how are you today?
**B:** I’m great! And you?
**A:** I’m good. Are you ready for class?
**B:** Yes, I am.

---

# 3. Talking at Work

**A:** Hello, how are you?
**B:** I’m very busy, but I’m okay.
**A:** That’s good to hear.
**B:** How about you?
**A:** I’m doing well.

---

# 4. Meeting a Neighbor

**A:** Hello, how are you?
**B:** I’m fine, thanks.
**A:** How is your family?
**B:** They are all good.

---

# 5. Phone Conversation

**A:** Hello, how are you?
**B:** Hi! I’m good. What about you?
**A:** I’m okay. What are you doing?
**B:** I’m watching TV.

---

# 6. Seeing a Classmate

**A:** Hello, how are you?
**B:** I’m a little tired today.
**A:** Oh, why?
**B:** I studied late last night.

---

# 7. At a Coffee Shop

**A:** Hello, how are you?
**B:** I’m happy today.
**A:** That’s nice!
**B:** Yes, I got good news.

---

# 8. Talking After the Weekend

**A:** Hello, how are you?
**B:** I’m good. I had a nice weekend.
**A:** Really? What did you do?
**B:** I visited my grandparents.

---

# 9. Meeting a New Friend

**A:** Hello, how are you?
**B:** I’m fine, thank you. Nice to meet you.
**A:** Nice to meet you too.
**B:** Where are you from?

---

# 10. Short Simple Conversation

**A:** Hello, how are you?
**B:** I’m good.
**A:** Great!
**B:** Thank you.
