# At the Airport — Sample Conversations

## Conversation 1: Check-in Counter

**Staff:** Good morning. Where are you flying today?
**Passenger:** I’m flying to Singapore.
**Staff:** May I see your passport, please?
**Passenger:** Sure. Here it is.
**Staff:** Do you have any bags to check in?
**Passenger:** Yes, one suitcase.
**Staff:** Okay. Your gate is A12. Boarding starts at 9:30.
**Passenger:** Thank you very much.
**Staff:** Have a nice flight!

---

## Conversation 2: Security Check

**Officer:** Please put your bag on the belt.
**Passenger:** Okay.
**Officer:** Do you have any liquids or laptops?
**Passenger:** Yes, I have a laptop.
**Officer:** Please take it out of your bag.
**Passenger:** No problem.
**Officer:** Thank you. You can walk through now.
**Passenger:** Thank you.

---

## Conversation 3: Asking for the Gate

**Traveler:** Excuse me, where is gate B15?
**Airport Staff:** Go straight and turn left.
**Traveler:** Is it far?
**Airport Staff:** No, about five minutes from here.
**Traveler:** Thank you for your help.
**Airport Staff:** You’re welcome.

---

## Conversation 4: At Immigration

**Officer:** What is the purpose of your visit?
**Traveler:** I’m here for vacation.
**Officer:** How long will you stay?
**Traveler:** For one week.
**Officer:** Where will you stay?
**Traveler:** At a hotel in the city center.
**Officer:** Okay. Enjoy your trip.
**Traveler:** Thank you.

---

## Conversation 5: Boarding the Plane

**Staff:** May I see your boarding pass?
**Passenger:** Yes, here it is.
**Staff:** Thank you. You may board now.
**Passenger:** Which seat is 18A?
**Staff:** It’s by the window on the left side.
**Passenger:** Great, thank you.
**Staff:** Have a pleasant flight.

---

## Conversation 6: Lost Luggage

**Passenger:** Excuse me, I can’t find my suitcase.
**Staff:** What color is your suitcase?
**Passenger:** It’s black and large.
**Staff:** Can you show me your baggage tag?
**Passenger:** Yes, here it is.
**Staff:** Okay, we will check it for you.
**Passenger:** Thank you.
**Staff:** Please wait a moment.

---

## Conversation 7: Buying Food at the Airport

**Cashier:** Hello. What would you like?
**Traveler:** I’d like a sandwich and a coffee.
**Cashier:** Small or large coffee?
**Traveler:** Large, please.
**Cashier:** Anything else?
**Traveler:** No, that’s all.
**Cashier:** That will be ten dollars.
**Traveler:** Here you are.

---

## Conversation 8: Asking About Flight Delay

**Passenger:** Excuse me, is flight VN220 delayed?
**Staff:** Yes, it is delayed by one hour.
**Passenger:** What time will it depart?
**Staff:** Around 6:45 PM.
**Passenger:** Okay, thank you.
**Staff:** Sorry for the inconvenience.

---

## Conversation 9: Meeting Someone at the Airport

**Friend 1:** Hi! Welcome to Vietnam!
**Friend 2:** Thank you! It’s great to see you.
**Friend 1:** How was your flight?
**Friend 2:** It was good but very long.
**Friend 1:** Are you tired?
**Friend 2:** A little bit.
**Friend 1:** Let’s go get some food.
**Friend 2:** Sounds great!

---

## Conversation 10: Taxi Outside the Airport

**Driver:** Hello, where do you want to go?
**Traveler:** To the city center, please.
**Driver:** Okay. Do you have a hotel name?
**Traveler:** Yes, Sunrise Hotel.
**Driver:** No problem. Please get in.
**Traveler:** How long will it take?
**Driver:** About thirty minutes.
