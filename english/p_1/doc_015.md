# 1. Having Dinner with My Family

**Mom:** Dinner is ready.
**Son:** Okay, I’m coming.
**Dad:** How was school today?
**Son:** It was good.

---

# 2. Watching TV Together

**Sister:** What are we watching tonight?
**Brother:** Let’s watch a comedy movie.
**Mom:** Good idea.
**Dad:** I’ll make some popcorn.

---

# 3. Talking in the Morning

**Mom:** Good morning, everyone.
**Daughter:** Good morning, Mom.
**Dad:** Did you sleep well?
**Daughter:** Yes, I did.

---

# 4. Helping at Home

**Mom:** Can you help me clean the table?
**Son:** Sure, Mom.
**Mom:** Thank you.
**Son:** You’re welcome.

---

# 5. Planning a Trip

**Dad:** Where should we go this weekend?
**Sister:** Let’s go to the beach.
**Mom:** That sounds fun.
**Brother:** I agree!

---

# 6. Talking About School

**Dad:** Do you have homework today?
**Son:** Yes, a lot of homework.
**Dad:** Need any help?
**Son:** Maybe with math.

---

# 7. Celebrating a Birthday

**Family:** Happy birthday!
**Birthday Girl:** Thank you so much!
**Mom:** Make a wish.
**Birthday Girl:** Okay!

---

# 8. Cooking Together

**Mom:** Can you cut the vegetables?
**Daughter:** Sure.
**Mom:** Be careful with the knife.
**Daughter:** Don’t worry.

---

# 9. Saying Goodnight

**Mom:** It’s late. Time for bed.
**Child:** Okay, Mom.
**Dad:** Goodnight.
**Child:** Goodnight.

---

# 10. Family Game Night

**Brother:** Let’s play a board game.
**Sister:** Great idea!
**Dad:** I want to play too.
**Mom:** This will be fun.
