# Tai lieu hoc AWS cho ky thi

## Tu duy cot loi
- Thuc hanh nhieu se gioi hon.
- Khong nen thi qua som neu chua tu tin.
- Kinh nghiem lam thuc te quan trong hon hoc thuoc.
- Neu can, hoc lai khoa hoc theo nhieu tuan, khong nen nhoi trong vai ngay.

## Cach thuc hanh nen lam
1. Trien khai mot ung dung san co len EC2 theo cach thu cong.
2. Trien khai ung dung do len Elastic Beanstalk va cau hinh Auto Scaling.
3. Tao pipeline CI/CD (CodeCommit, CodeBuild, CodeDeploy, CodePipeline).
4. Tach ung dung thanh nhieu thanh phan va dung SQS, SNS de giam phu thuoc.
5. Thu phien ban serverless voi Lambda + API Gateway + DynamoDB.

## Du an nho de luyen tay
- Viet script (CLI/SDK) de tat EC2 ban dem va mo lai buoi sang.
- Tu dong tao snapshot EBS hang ngay.
- Liet ke cac EC2 su dung kem hieu qua (vi du CPU < 10%).

## Chien luoc lam bai thi
- Bat dau bang cach loai cac dap an chac chan sai.
- Da so cau hoi theo tinh huong thuc te.
- Khong overthink: de thi rat it cau danh do.
- Uu tien dap an don gian, hop ly ve van hanh.
- Neu co 2 dap an deu dung, chon dap an it phuc tap, it ton cong hon.
- Chu y tu khoa (vi du "serverless" thuong lien quan Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB).

## Ke hoach hoc 4 tuan (goi y)
### Tuan 1
- IAM, EC2, EBS, VPC co ban.
- Trien khai 1 app len EC2 bang tay.

### Tuan 2
- Elastic Beanstalk, Auto Scaling, ELB.
- Tao 1 pipeline CI/CD.

### Tuan 3
- SQS, SNS, Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB.
- Refactor 1 luong xu ly sang serverless.

### Tuan 4
- On lai phan yeu, FAQ, whitepaper.
- Lam de thi thu va phan tich loi sai.

## Loi khuyen cuoi
- Xay dung su tu tin bang trien khai thuc te.
- Tap trung vao quyet dinh thiet ke kien truc co tinh ung dung.
- Hoc deu va giu nhip on dinh truoc ngay thi.
