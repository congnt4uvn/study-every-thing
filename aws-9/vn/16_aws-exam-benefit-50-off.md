# Meo thi AWS: Giam 50% cho ky thi tiep theo

## Tong quan
Sau khi ban dau mot ky thi chung chi AWS, AWS co the cap benefit token giup ban duoc **giam 50%** cho mot ky thi AWS tiep theo.

## Tim benefit o dau
1. Dang nhap tai khoan AWS Certification.
2. Kiem tra muc benefits/tokens.
3. Tim cac benefit co trang thai **Unclaimed**.
4. Kiem tra **ngay het han** truoc khi su dung.

## Cach claim benefit
1. Bam **Claim Benefits**.
2. Sau khi claim, trang thai se chuyen thanh **Claimed**.
3. He thong se cung cap **ma voucher**.

## Cach ap dung ma voucher
1. Them ky thi vao gio hang.
2. Bam **Proceed to Checkout**.
3. O buoc thanh toan, tim o nhap **voucher/promo code**.
4. Dan ma voucher da claim.
5. Ap dung ma va xac nhan so tien da duoc giam.

## Ghi chu nhanh
- Muc giam 50% ap dung cho ky thi AWS hop le tiep theo.
- Benefit de trang thai unclaimed co the het han, nen dung som.
- Luon kiem tra tong chi phi truoc khi thanh toan.

## Nhac nho hoc tap
De tiet kiem chi phi, hay lap ke hoach thi:
- Dau 1 chung chi.
- Claim token giam gia.
- Dung token cho ky thi chung chi tiep theo.
