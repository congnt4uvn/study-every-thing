# Ghi Chu Hoc AWS: Tong Quan Phan Cuoi

## Phan nay noi ve gi

Day la **phan cuoi dac biet** cua khoa hoc.
Phan nay gioi thieu mot so cong nghe AWS bo sung co the xuat hien trong de thi.

## Ky vong trong bai thi

- Cac cau hoi tu nhom chu de nay thuong **rat co ban**.
- Thuong chi ro vao **mot hoac hai cau hoi**.
- Ban nen tap trung vao **hieu tong quan**, khong can dao sau ky thuat.

## Pham vi bai giang

Bai giang **khong dao sau** tung dich vu.
No cung cap tong quan nhanh ve nhieu dich vu va khai niem.

## Vi sao van quan trong

De thi AWS co the thay doi theo thoi gian nen noi dung co the duoc cap nhat.
Giang vien cho biet se bo sung bai giang phu hop khi can.

## Dieu can nho

Tat ca kien thuc can cho ky thi hien tai da duoc de cap.
Ban nen:

- On tap ky cac dich vu AWS cot loi truoc.
- Dung phan nay de cung co cac chu de bo sung.
- Nho rang muc do kiem tra thuong la co ban.

## Checklist on tap nhanh

- [ ] Minh hieu day la phan tong quan.
- [ ] Minh nhan biet duoc ten va muc dich cua cac dich vu AWS bo sung.
- [ ] Minh san sang cho cac cau hoi co ban tu nhom chu de nay.
- [ ] Minh uu tien dich vu cot loi nhung van on phan nay.
