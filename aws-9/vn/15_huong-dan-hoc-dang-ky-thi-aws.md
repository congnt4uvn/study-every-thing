# Tai lieu hoc dang ky thi AWS Certification

## 1. Dang nhap AWS Certification
- Truy cap: `aws.training/certification`
- Bam **Sign In**.
- Tao **AWS Builder ID** neu ban chua co.
- Sau khi dang nhap, bam **Go to Your Account** de vao cong thong tin chung chi.

## 2. Cac muc quan trong trong portal
- Muc chinh de dung ngay: **Exam Registration**.
- Nen xem them:
- **Exam accommodations** (ho tro them thoi gian cho nguoi khong phai ban ngu tieng Anh).
- **Certification benefits** (co the co uu dai, vi du giam 50% cho lan thi tiep theo).

## 3. Bat dau dat lich thi
- Vao **Exam Registration** va chon **Schedule an exam**.
- Tim bai thi muon dang ky (vi du: **AWS Certified Developer - Associate**).
- Neu can, bam **Authorize** truoc, sau do bam **Schedule**.
- He thong se chuyen sang cong Pearson VUE.

## 4. Chon hinh thuc thi

### Lua chon A: Thi tai test center (truc tiep)
- Thi tai dia diem duoc uy quyen.
- Mang theo giay to tuy than co anh.
- Xem ky cac quy dinh ve vat dung duoc/khong duoc mang vao phong thi.
- Tiep tuc chon ngon ngu, dia diem, ngay gio thi.

### Lua chon B: Thi online voi OnVUE (tai nha)
- Can co:
- May tinh co webcam.
- Ket noi internet on dinh.
- Bam **Run system test** som truoc ngay thi.
- Don dep ban hoc/khong gian thi (giam thi se yeu cau kiem tra).
- Chuan bi giay to tuy than.
- Check-in som khoang **30 phut** truoc gio hen.

## 5. Hoan tat dat lich
- Chon ngon ngu bai thi.
- Dong y cac dieu khoan bat buoc.
- Chon ngon ngu giao tiep voi nguoi check-in/giam thi.
- Chon mui gio.
- Chon ngay va gio thi.
- Thanh toan le phi.
- Nhap ma voucher neu co ma giam gia.

## 6. Vao thi trong ngay thi
- Trong portal AWS Certification, vao **Home / Dashboard**.
- Bam **Manage my Pearson VUE account**.
- Tim bai thi da dat lich va bam **Launch exam** khi den gio.

## Checklist truoc ngay thi
- [ ] Da tao va xac minh AWS Builder ID.
- [ ] Da dat lich thi va nhan xac nhan.
- [ ] Da chon hinh thuc thi (test center hoac online OnVUE).
- [ ] Da chay system test (neu thi online).
- [ ] Da chuan bi giay to tuy than co anh.
- [ ] Ban hoc/khong gian thi da duoc don gon.
- [ ] Da san sang voucher (neu co).
- [ ] Ke hoach check-in som 30 phut.
