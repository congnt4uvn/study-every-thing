# AWS Exam Tip: Get 50% Off Your Next Exam

## Overview
After you pass an AWS certification exam, AWS may grant a benefit token that gives you **50% off** a future AWS exam.

## Where to Find the Benefit
1. Sign in to your AWS Certification account.
2. Check your available benefits/tokens.
3. Look for benefits marked as **Unclaimed**.
4. Verify the **expiry date** before using the benefit.

## How to Claim the Benefit
1. Click **Claim Benefits**.
2. After claiming, the status changes to **Claimed**.
3. A **voucher code** will be provided.

## How to Apply the Voucher Code
1. Add your exam to the cart.
2. Click **Proceed to Checkout**.
3. At payment, find the **voucher/promo code** field.
4. Paste the claimed voucher code.
5. Apply the code and confirm the discount on total cost.

## Quick Notes
- The 50% discount applies to your next eligible AWS exam.
- Unclaimed benefits can expire, so claim and use them on time.
- Always verify the final total before payment.

## Study Reminder
Save money by planning your exam sequence:
- Pass one exam.
- Claim the discount token.
- Use it for your next certification exam.
