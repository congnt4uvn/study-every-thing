# AWS Certification Exam Registration Study Guide

## 1. Sign in to AWS Certification
- Go to: `aws.training/certification`
- Click **Sign In**.
- Create an **AWS Builder ID** if you do not already have one.
- After signing in, click **Go to Your Account** to open the certification portal.

## 2. Explore important portal options
- Main area to use now: **Exam Registration**.
- Also review:
- **Exam accommodations** (useful for non-native English speakers, may provide extra time).
- **Certification benefits** (possible discounts, such as 50% off a future exam).

## 3. Start scheduling an exam
- Open **Exam Registration** and choose **Schedule an exam**.
- Find your target exam (example: **AWS Certified Developer - Associate**).
- If needed, click **Authorize** first, then click **Schedule**.
- You will be redirected to the **Pearson VUE** portal.

## 4. Choose exam delivery method

### Option A: Test Center (In-person)
- Take the exam at an official test center.
- Bring valid photo ID.
- Review test center rules about allowed and prohibited items.
- Continue with language, location, date, and time selection.

### Option B: Online with OnVUE (At home)
- Requires:
- Computer with webcam.
- Stable internet connection.
- Click **Run system test** well before exam day.
- Clear your desk and testing space (proctor will check your environment).
- Bring valid ID.
- Check in about **30 minutes early**.

## 5. Complete booking details
- Choose exam language.
- Accept required terms and conditions.
- Select communication language for check-in/proctoring.
- Set your time zone.
- Select date and time.
- Complete payment.
- Apply voucher code if you have a discount.

## 6. Launch your exam on test day
- In AWS Certification portal, go to **Home / Dashboard**.
- Click **Manage my Pearson VUE account**.
- Find your scheduled exam and click **Launch exam** when it is time.

## Quick checklist before exam day
- [ ] AWS Builder ID created and verified.
- [ ] Exam scheduled and confirmed.
- [ ] Delivery mode chosen (Test Center or Online OnVUE).
- [ ] System test completed (for online exam).
- [ ] Valid photo ID prepared.
- [ ] Desk/testing area cleaned.
- [ ] Voucher code ready (if available).
- [ ] Check-in plan: 30 minutes early.
