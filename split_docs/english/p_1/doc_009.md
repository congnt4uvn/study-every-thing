# 1. Because I Was Tired



**Mom:** Why did you go to bed early?

**Son:** Because I was tired.

**Mom:** Did you have a busy day?

**Son:** Yes, very busy.



---



# 2. Because It Was Raining



**Anna:** Why didn’t you go outside?

**Lisa:** Because it was raining.

**Anna:** Oh, I see.

**Lisa:** Maybe tomorrow.



---



# 3. Because I Love Music



**Tom:** Why do you practice guitar every day?

**Ben:** Because I love music.

**Tom:** That’s great.

**Ben:** It makes me happy.



---



# 4. Because I Was Hungry



**Dad:** Why did you eat so much?

**Son:** Because I was hungry.

**Dad:** Did you skip lunch?

**Son:** Yes, I did.



---



# 5. Because English Is Important



**Teacher:** Why do you study English?

**Student:** Because English is important.

**Teacher:** That’s true.

**Student:** I want to speak fluently.



---



# 6. Because She Is Kind



**Emma:** Why do you like your teacher?

**Lucy:** Because she is kind.

**Emma:** That’s nice.

**Lucy:** She always helps us.



---



# 7. Because I Like Sports



**Coach:** Why do you run every morning?

**Ryan:** Because I like sports.

**Coach:** Good habit!

**Ryan:** Thank you.

# 8. Because It Is Delicious



**Mom:** Why do you like this cake?

# 9. Because I Want to Learn



**Friend:** Why do you read so many books?

**Friend:** That’s a good reason.

# 10. Because It Makes Me Happy



**Jenny:** Why do you paint every day?

**Jenny:** Your paintings are beautiful.
