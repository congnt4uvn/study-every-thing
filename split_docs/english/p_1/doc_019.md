# 1. Describing Pizza



**Anna:** What did you eat for lunch?

**Ben:** I had a big slice of pizza.

**Anna:** How was it?

**Ben:** It was hot, cheesy, and very delicious.

**Anna:** What toppings did it have?

**Ben:** Pepperoni, mushrooms, and olives.

**Anna:** Sounds tasty!

# 2. Describing Ice Cream



**Lucy:** What flavor of ice cream do you like?

**Tom:** I like chocolate ice cream.

**Lucy:** Why do you like it?

**Tom:** It is sweet, cold, and creamy.

**Lucy:** Do you eat it often?

**Tom:** Yes, especially in summer.

# 3. Describing Vietnamese Pho



**Mai:** Did you eat pho this morning?

**Nam:** Yes, I did.

**Mai:** How was it?

**Nam:** The soup was hot and flavorful.

**Mai:** What was inside?

**Nam:** Beef, noodles, and fresh herbs.

**Mai:** I love pho too!

# 4. Describing Fried Chicken



**David:** What is your favorite fast food?

**Sarah:** Fried chicken.

**David:** Can you describe it?

**Sarah:** It is crispy outside and juicy inside.

**David:** Is it spicy?

**Sarah:** A little spicy, but very tasty.

# 5. Describing a Hamburger



**Kevin:** What did you order?

**Linda:** I ordered a hamburger.

**Kevin:** Was it good?

**Linda:** Yes, it was big and delicious.

**Kevin:** What was in it?

**Linda:** Beef, cheese, lettuce, and tomato.

# 6. Describing Salad



**Emily:** Are you eating healthy food?

**Jack:** Yes, I made a salad.

**Emily:** What does it taste like?

**Jack:** It is fresh and crunchy.

**Emily:** What vegetables did you use?

**Jack:** Lettuce, cucumber, and carrots.

# 7. Describing Sushi



**Chris:** Have you tried sushi before?

**Olivia:** Yes, I have.

**Chris:** Do you like it?

**Olivia:** Yes, especially salmon sushi.

# 9. Describing Spaghetti



**Peter:** What are you cooking?

**Jane:** Spaghetti with tomato sauce.

**Peter:** Is it good?

**Jane:** No, it is savory and rich.

# 10. Describing Fruit



**Amy:** What fruit do you like most?

**John:** I like mangoes.

**Amy:** Do you eat them often?

**John:** Yes, almost every week.
