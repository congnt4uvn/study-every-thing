# 1. Ordering Coffee



**Anna:** What would you like to drink?

**Lisa:** I’d like a latte.

**Anna:** I’ll get a cappuccino.

**Lisa:** Great choice.



---



# 2. Talking About Work



**Tom:** How’s your new job?

**Ben:** It’s going well.

**Tom:** Do you like your coworkers?

**Ben:** Yes, they are very friendly.



---



# 3. Studying Together



**Emma:** Did you finish the homework?

**Lucy:** Not yet.

**Emma:** Let’s study together.

**Lucy:** Good idea.



---



# 4. Weekend Plans



**Ryan:** What are you doing this weekend?

**Jack:** I’m going to the beach.

**Ryan:** Sounds fun!

**Jack:** You should come too.



---



# 5. Talking About Movies



**Mia:** Did you watch the new movie?

**Jenny:** Yes, I watched it yesterday.

**Mia:** Was it good?

**Jenny:** Yes, it was amazing.



---



# 6. Meeting After a Long Time



**Kevin:** It’s been a long time!

**Chris:** I know! How have you been?

**Kevin:** Pretty good.

**Chris:** I’m happy to see you again.



---



# 7. Talking About Food



**Anna:** This cake is delicious.

**Lisa:** Yes, I love it.

**Anna:** Do you want another piece?

**Lisa:** Maybe just one more.



---



# 8. Talking About English



**Student 1:** How do you practice English?

**Student 2:** I watch English videos online.

**Student 1:** That’s a good idea.

**Student 2:** It helps me learn new words.

# 9. Taking Photos



**Lucy:** Let’s take a picture together.

**Emma:** Cheese!

# 10. Saying Goodbye



**Tom:** I have to go now.

**Ben:** Okay, see you later.
