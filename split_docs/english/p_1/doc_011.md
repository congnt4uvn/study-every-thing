# 1. Checking In at a Hotel



**Receptionist:** Good evening. Welcome to the hotel.

**Guest:** Good evening. I have a reservation.

**Receptionist:** What is your name, please?

**Guest:** My name is John Smith.



---



# 2. Asking for a Room



**Guest:** Do you have any free rooms?

**Receptionist:** Yes, we do.

**Guest:** How much is one night?

**Receptionist:** It is 50 dollars per night.



---



# 3. Asking About Breakfast



**Guest:** Is breakfast included?

**Receptionist:** Yes, breakfast is included.

**Guest:** What time is breakfast?

**Receptionist:** From 7 a.m. to 10 a.m.



---



# 4. Asking for Wi-Fi



**Guest:** What is the Wi-Fi password?

**Receptionist:** The password is written on your key card.

**Guest:** Thank you.

**Receptionist:** You’re welcome.



---



# 5. Calling Room Service



**Guest:** Hello, I would like some water.

**Staff:** Sure. Anything else?

**Guest:** No, thank you.

**Staff:** We will bring it soon.



---



# 6. Asking for Towels



**Guest:** Can I have extra towels?

**Receptionist:** Of course.

**Guest:** Thank you very much.

**Receptionist:** No problem.



---



# 7. Asking About Check-Out Time



**Guest:** What time is check-out?

**Receptionist:** Check-out is at 12 p.m.

**Guest:** Okay, thank you.

**Receptionist:** Have a nice stay.



---



# 8. Asking for Directions



**Guest:** Where is the elevator?

**Staff:** It is next to the restaurant.

**Guest:** Thank you.

**Staff:** You’re welcome.



---



# 9. Reporting a Problem



**Guest:** The air conditioner is not working.

**Receptionist:** I’m sorry about that.

**Guest:** Can someone fix it?

**Receptionist:** Yes, maintenance will come soon.



---



# 10. Checking Out



**Receptionist:** Did you enjoy your stay?

**Guest:** Yes, very much.

**Receptionist:** Thank you for staying with us.

**Guest:** Thank you. Goodbye.
