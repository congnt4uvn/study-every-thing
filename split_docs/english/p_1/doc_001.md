## Simple Grammar Patterns

### Present Simple

* I play football.
* She likes cats.

### Present Continuous

* I am studying now.
* They are watching TV.

### Past Simple

* I visited my friend yesterday.
* We watched a movie last night.

### Future

* I will call you later.
* I am going to travel tomorrow.

### Can / Can’t

* I can swim.
* I can’t drive.

### Want to

* I want to learn English.
* She wants to buy a phone.

### Like + Verb-ing

* I like reading books.
* They like playing games.
