# 1. Arriving at the Restaurant



**Waiter:** Good evening. Welcome to the restaurant.

**Customer:** Good evening.

**Waiter:** How many people?

**Customer:** Two people, please.



---



# 2. Ordering Food



**Waiter:** Are you ready to order?

**Customer:** Yes, I’d like a burger and fries.

**Waiter:** Anything to drink?

**Customer:** A cola, please.



---



# 3. Asking About the Menu



**Customer:** What do you recommend?

**Waiter:** Our chicken pasta is very popular.

**Customer:** Okay, I’ll try it.

**Waiter:** Great choice.



---



# 4. Ordering Drinks



**Waiter:** What would you like to drink?

**Customer:** I’d like orange juice.

**Waiter:** Small or large?

**Customer:** Large, please.



---



# 5. Asking for the Bill



**Customer:** Excuse me, can I have the bill?

**Waiter:** Of course. I’ll bring it right away.

**Customer:** Thank you.

**Waiter:** You’re welcome.



---



# 6. Talking About the Food



**Anna:** The food is delicious.

**Lisa:** Yes, I really like the soup.

**Anna:** The service is good too.

**Lisa:** I agree.



---



# 7. Making a Reservation



**Customer:** I’d like to reserve a table.

**Receptionist:** For how many people?

**Customer:** Four people.

**Receptionist:** What time would you like?



---



# 8. Asking for More Water



**Customer:** Could we have more water, please?

**Waiter:** Sure, no problem.

**Customer:** Thank you very much.

**Waiter:** My pleasure.



---



# 9. At a Fast Food Restaurant



**Cashier:** May I take your order?

**Customer:** Yes, one cheeseburger, please.

**Cashier:** Would you like fries with that?

**Customer:** Yes, please.



---



# 10. Leaving the Restaurant



**Waiter:** Thank you for coming.

**Customer:** Thank you. Everything was great.

**Waiter:** Have a nice evening.

**Customer:** You too.
